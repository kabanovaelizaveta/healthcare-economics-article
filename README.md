# Analysis of Digital Tools’ Usage Economic Aspect in The Healthcare Sector in Ukraine: Global Context and Wartime Adaptation

## Abstract
The article investigates the economic and scientific potential of digital health tools in Ukraine, focusing on their resilience and adaptation in crisis conditions. The study compares Ukraine’s digital health system with global trends, highlighting standards, wartime challenges, and development opportunities. Methods include narrative review, statistical analysis of economic sectors, and evaluation of digitalization under wartime conditions. Results show that, although Ukraine lags behind global leaders, it has demonstrated cohesion and adaptability in response to COVID-19 and war. The implementation of several key digital services reflects resilience and readiness for global integration. The study concludes that Ukraine’s digital health sector has significant economic potential, contingent on international cooperation, robust governance, data protection, and clear regulations, enabling system improvements and accelerated integration into global digital health networks.
